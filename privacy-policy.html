Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

InPay collects various types of information to provide and improve our payment gateway services. This includes personal identification information (such as name, email address, mailing address, phone number, company name) when you register for an account, subscribe to our newsletter, fill out a form, or interact with our services. We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us. You can always refuse to supply personal identification information, though it may prevent you from engaging in certain service-related activities.

2. How We Use Cookies

InPay uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and for security purposes. Cookies help us remember your preferences, facilitate secure logins, and provide personalized content. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling them may affect the functionality of our services.

  • Essential cookies for site functionality.
  • Analytical cookies to understand user behavior.
  • Security cookies to protect against fraud.
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings.
  • Third-party cookies for integrated services.
  • You can manage cookie preferences in your browser.

3. The Collection, Process, and Use of Personal Data

InPay processes personal data to provide, maintain, and improve our services, process transactions, communicate with you, and ensure compliance with legal obligations. This includes collecting data necessary for identity verification, fraud prevention, and transaction processing. We use your data to facilitate seamless payment experiences and offer personalized support.

We may also use aggregated or anonymized data for analytical purposes to understand trends and improve our services, without identifying individual users.

4. Data Protection

InPay is committed to protecting your data. We implement robust security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure server facilities, to safeguard your personal and transactional information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We adhere to industry best practices and regulatory requirements for data protection.

Our security protocols are regularly reviewed and updated to ensure the highest level of protection for your data. We also ensure that any third-party service providers we engage meet our stringent security standards.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. The retention period varies depending on the type of data and its purpose. Once data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

  1. Transaction records are retained for compliance and auditing purposes.
  2. Account information is kept as long as your account is active.
  3. Marketing preferences are retained until you opt-out.
  4. Data is securely deleted or anonymized after the retention period.

6. Our Policy For Age Under 18

InPay's services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information from our files as soon as possible.
