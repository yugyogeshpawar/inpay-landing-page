Common Questions About InPay Payment Gateway.

Here you'll find answers to the most common questions about InPay's payment gateway services, features, and support. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact our support team.

InPay is a comprehensive payment gateway solution that helps businesses accept various forms of digital payments securely and efficiently. It enables merchants to process transactions from credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and mobile wallets, simplifying financial operations.

InPay processes payments by securely capturing customer details and routing them through various financial networks for authorization. Once approved, funds are transferred to your account, often with instant or T+1 settlements. Our platform provides a dashboard for tracking transactions and managing payouts.

InPay supports a wide range of payment methods, including major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, etc.), UPI, NetBanking, and popular mobile wallets. This ensures you can cater to a broad customer base and their preferred payment options.

Yes, InPay prioritizes security. We are PCI DSS compliant and employ advanced encryption and real-time fraud detection systems to protect all transactions and sensitive customer data. Your financial information is always safe with us.

InPay charges competitive and transparent fees per transaction, which may vary based on transaction volume and other factors. We also offer promotional deals for high-volume transactions. Please contact our sales team for detailed pricing information tailored to your business needs.

Currently, InPay primarily focuses on domestic transactions within India. However, we are actively working on expanding our services to include international transactions in the near future. Please stay tuned for updates.

InPay offers 24/7 customer support to assist with all your queries, troubleshooting, and general assistance. You can reach us via call, chat, or email for dedicated and effective help.

InPay provides a comprehensive merchant dashboard where you can easily track all transactions, view payment histories, and generate detailed reports. This feature helps you monitor your financial activities and reconcile payments efficiently.

Yes, InPay is designed for easy integration with various e-commerce platforms, CRM systems, accounting software, and other business tools. We provide developer-friendly APIs to ensure a seamless connection with your existing infrastructure, boosting overall financial management efficiency.

InPay – Smarter, Faster & Secure Payment Solutions InPay is a trusted digital payment gateway that empowers individuals and businesses with seamless financial transactions. Our mission is to simplify payments, reduce costs, and enhance security, so that you can focus on what matters the most – growth and success.
