DeepLink Payments

DeepLink allows your customers to make payments directly through a unique link without opening multiple apps or pages. With just one click, users can complete transactions smoothly, making the payment experience faster and more convenient.

Intent Flow

Intent-based payment flow helps in seamless mobile app transactions. Customers are redirected securely to their UPI or banking app, complete the payment, and return back to your application automatically. This ensures reliability, speed, and zero payment drop-offs.
